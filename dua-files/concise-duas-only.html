My 6 Dream Duas

For daily repetition in the last ten nights

1. Health
Ya Allah, make me reach 60kg—strong, energetic, glowing, pain-free. Give me strength for qiyam and fasting. Grant me the highest emotional intelligence and wisdom. Let me make good decisions and stick to them. Free me from overthinking. When I err, let me forgive myself as You forgive me. Make my body and mind tools for Your worship until my last breath.
2. Wealth
Ya Allah, grant me wealth in my hand, not my heart—$1M growing to $10M. Give me properties that pay themselves, businesses that thrive. Let me build a masjid where my sons lead as imams. Fund orphans, build wells, cut ribbons for charity. Grant us annual Umrah without counting cost. Make my wealth a bridge to Jannah, not a barrier.
3. Deen
Ya Allah, make me never miss tahajjud—standing before You in tears, longing for Your Face. Grant me khushu' that makes salah the coolness of my eyes. Let me author resources that reach millions and attach hearts to Your Quran. Make my home a place of daily Quran and family halaqa. Let me die with "La ilaha illAllah" on my tongue.
4. Spouse
Ya Allah, make us complement and complete each other. Let me support him like Khadija, shield him like Umm Sulaim, turn to You like Fatima. Heal his heart—replace harshness with gentleness, sadness with sakina. Let us walk through Madinah one day, old and gray, still in love. Reunite us in Jannatul Firdaus, together forever.
5. Children
Ya Allah, place the Quran in their hearts. Make them huffadh with beautiful akhlaaq—humble, wise, confident, unafraid to do right. Bond them like the brothers of Yusuf, without envy. Let me see them lead Eid prayer. Let me sit at their weddings, tears streaming with gratitude. Make them the coolness of my eyes here and my companions in Jannah.
6. Fun
Ya Allah, let us enjoy Your tayyibat without guilt. Grant us family Umrah every year, standing together at the Ka'bah. Give us beach holidays, mountain getaways, spa days, board game nights with laughter and no screens. Make our home a peaceful retreat we never want to leave. Let our joy be halal and our laughter echo with gratitude.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي

"O Allah, You are the Pardoner, You love to pardon, so pardon me."
